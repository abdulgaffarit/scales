Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

At USA Salaries, we respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected

When you visit our site, our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may automatically collect:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type and screen resolution

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone)
  • We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes
  • We do not sell any data to third parties

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for site functionality:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site operation (Cloudflare security)
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the site

Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare: Hosting and CDN — subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Typography — subject to Google's Privacy Policy

Advertising

If advertising is displayed on this site, it may use cookies to serve relevant ads. Ad partners may collect anonymous browsing data. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your browser settings or at aboutads.info.

Data Security

Our site is served over HTTPS with TLS encryption. We use Cloudflare's security features including DDoS protection and Web Application Firewall.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you (we hold none)
  • Request deletion of any data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking via browser settings
  • Use ad-blocking software

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please visit our contact page.
